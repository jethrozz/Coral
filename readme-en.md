Corail & VaultBridge: The Permanent Home for Your Obsidian Vault
The Problem: Your ideas in Obsidian are powerful, but they're trapped locally or in vulnerable cloud services. You lack a way to permanently preserve your work and unlock its value for a wider audience.

Our Solution: A seamless ecosystem that bridges your personal knowledge management in Obsidian with the permanence and economy of Web3.

VaultBridge: Your Gateway to Permanence
VaultBridge is a dedicated plugin for Obsidian. With a single click, it securely synchronizes your notes and articles from your local Obsidian vault directly to the Walrus network, a permanent, decentralized storage layer built on the Arweave ecosystem.

For the Obsidian User: This is the ultimate backup and preservation tool. It transforms your private notes into immutable, permanent digital artifacts, ensuring they can never be lost, censored, or taken down.

Corail: Where Your Ideas Grow Value
Corail is a premium subscription platform built on top of this permanent content layer. When you sync an article via VaultBridge, Corail automatically generates a beautiful, public page for it, ready for audience discovery and subscription.

For the Obsidian User: Corail effortlessly turns your Obsidian vault into a professional publishing hub. You can curate specific notes into paid newsletters or knowledge bases, allowing your readers to support your work directly through transparent crypto payments.

How They Work Together: A Seamless Workflow
The magic happens in the connection. VaultBridge serves as the critical pipeline, and Corail is the destination.

Create in Obsidian: You write and refine your content using the powerful, familiar Markdown environment of Obsidian.

Sync with VaultBridge: You publish directly from Obsidian to the permanent Web3 storage with one click using the VaultBridge plugin.

Thrive on Corail: Your permanently stored article instantly becomes a subscribable asset on the Corail platform, enabling you to build a community and generate revenue.

In essence, we extend the power of Obsidian. You continue to work in the tool you love, but now with the superpowers of permanent, uncensorable storage and a direct path to monetizing your knowledge.
